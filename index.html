About SnugBrew

SnugBrew crafts non-caffeinated ritual kits that pair scent, micro-skincare, and soft ritual accessories to create 5–15 minute reset routines. Each kit contains a tin, seasonal scent sachets, a single-use eye mask, a micro skincare sachet, and a guided ritual card. We use ethically sourced ingredients and biodegradable packaging. Made with care in Chennai, India.
